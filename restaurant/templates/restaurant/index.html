About us

In 1997, nestled within a quiet forest, a chef transformed a simple childhood treehouse into a unique treetop restaurant. Once a secret spot of joy, it was refurbished in 2021, and it’s now a cozy dining place where delicious meals bring back sweet memories among rustling leaves. With a mix of rustic charm and tasty dishes, it offers a memorable dining experience under the stars.
